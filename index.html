Background:

My name is Fall McCoy, I study writing and practice both professional and creative styles.

Storytelling is my greatest artistic passion, as I love crafting tales of tragedy, drama, horror, psychological exploration, sci-fi, action, and other genres as well as mixing some together.

Since storytelling is such a big passion of mine, I make sure to experience a lot of art and entertainment from various mediums. This is both because I love consuming art and entertainment, but also so that I can get inspiration and analyze how other writers craft their stories.
